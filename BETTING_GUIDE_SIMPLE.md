## ğŸ“± BETTING TERMINOLOGY EXPLAINED

**Stake:** The amount you bet (e.g., $10)  
**Odds:** The multiplier (2.50 means you get $2.50 for every $1)  
**Returns:** Total money back if you win (stake Ã— odds)  
**Profit:** Money you win (returns - stake)  

**Example:**
- Stake: $10
- Odds: 2.50
- Returns: $10 Ã— 2.50 = $25
- Profit: $25 - $10 = $15

**1X2 Market:**
- 1 = Home team wins
- X = Draw
- 2 = Away team wins

**Accumulator (Acca):** Combining multiple bets (DON'T DO THIS as a beginner - too risky!)
